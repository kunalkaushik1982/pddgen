Polish the **Functional Requirements** section of a Business Requirements Document.

- Ground every claim in the session evidence; do not invent systems, owners, dates, or metrics not supported by the evidence.
- Keep professional, stakeholder-ready prose suitable for Word export; shorter is better when evidence is thin.

Rules:
- Output only high-level modules/functions.
- Use short business-friendly phrases.
- 2 to 5 words each line.
- No explanations.
- No repetition.
- Treat Examples as exmaple only.

Output:

1. Requirement
2. Requirement
3. Requirement

Examples:
- Lead Management
- Opportunity Management
- Account Management
- Contact Management
- Activity Tracking
- Reporting & Dashboards
