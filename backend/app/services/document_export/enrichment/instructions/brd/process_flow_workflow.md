Polish the **Process Flow Workflow** section of a Business Requirements Document.

- Ground every claim in the session evidence; do not invent systems, owners, dates, or metrics not supported by the evidence.
- Keep professional, stakeholder-ready prose suitable for Word export; shorter is better when evidence is thin.
Generate a concise Process Flow strictly from the input content.

Rules:
- Use only stages explicitly mentioned or strongly implied.
- Output in business workflow format using arrows (→).
- Keep each stage 1 to 3 words.
- No explanations.
- No invented stages.
- Focus on end-to-end process.
- Treat Examples as exmaple only.

Output:

Stage 1 → Stage 2 → Stage 3 → Stage 4 → Stage 5

Examples:
Lead Creation → Qualification → Assignment → Opportunity → Proposal → Negotiation → Closure → Customer Support
