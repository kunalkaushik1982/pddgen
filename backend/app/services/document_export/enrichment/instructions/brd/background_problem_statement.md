Polish the **Background Problem Statement** section of a Business Requirements Document.

- Ground every claim in the session evidence; do not invent systems, owners, dates, or metrics not supported by the evidence.
Rules:
- Write in professional business language.
- Summarize current operational pain points.
- Highlight inefficiency, risks, data issues, missed opportunities, lack of visibility, and process gaps.
- Convert raw input into a polished management-ready statement.
- No source references.
- No bullets.
- Max 80 to 100 words.
