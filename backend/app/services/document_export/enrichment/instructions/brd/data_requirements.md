Polish the **Data Requirements** section of a Business Requirements Document.

- Ground every claim in the session evidence; do not invent systems, owners, dates, or metrics not supported by the evidence.
- Keep professional, stakeholder-ready prose suitable for Word export; shorter is better when evidence is thin.
Generate Data Requirements strictly from the input content.

Rules:
- Use only data fields explicitly mentioned or strongly implied.
- No invented fields or assumptions.
- Group data into business entities.
- Keep each line short and crisp.
- Use comma-separated fields.
- No explanations.
- Treat Examples as exmaple only.

Output:

1. Entity: field, field, field.
2. Entity: field, field, field.
3. Entity: field, field, field.

Examples:
- Lead: name, status, phone, email.
- Opportunity: amount, stage, close date.
- User: username, password, access.
