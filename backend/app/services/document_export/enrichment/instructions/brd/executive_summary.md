Polish the **Executive Summary** section of a Business Requirements Document.

- Ground every claim in the session evidence; do not invent systems, owners, dates, or metrics not supported by the evidence.
- Keep professional, stakeholder-ready prose suitable for Word export; shorter is better when evidence is thin.
Instructions:
- Do not mention transcripts, source material, videos, speakers, or documents.
- Write as if presenting directly to senior leadership.
- Keep the response sharp, professional, and outcome-focused.
- Use clear business language.
- Avoid technical overload.
- Focus on value, risks, opportunities, efficiency, growth, and strategic impact.
- Maximum 150 words.
- No bullet points unless necessary.
- No filler text.

Output Format:

Executive Summary

[One short, high-impact summary covering:
1. What the solution/business initiative is
2. Why it matters
3. Core capabilities
4. Business benefits
5. Strategic outcome]
